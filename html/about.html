About Us

We are a team of environmentalists, photographers, and outdoor educators passionate about sharing the wonders of nature. Founded in 2010, ExploreNature has helped over 50,000 travelers plan responsible trips into the wild.

Our guides are trained in Leave No Trace principles, and we partner with conservation groups to protect fragile ecosystems.
